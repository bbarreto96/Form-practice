VicNet

WHAT IS IT?

ONLINE VOLUNTEERS PORTAL

VOLUNTEERS CAN ACCESS

  • Their Profile/contact info
  • Their schedules
  • Their hours served
  • Schedule openings
  • Messages from you

SYSTEM OPERATORS ARE ABLE TO

  • View and manage schedules for volunteers
  • Control the degree and kinds of access volunters have

WHERE TO USE:

  • Anywhere through an internet-connected computer at home, work, or on the road

REQUIREMENTS:

  • A Volgistics account
